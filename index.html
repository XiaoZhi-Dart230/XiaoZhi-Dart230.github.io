The King's Last Journey


I followed my map that shows me the way, It radiates brilliance, shining like a guiding beacon.


I am well acquainted with Death. I have no fear of him, so my soul stubbornly presses on.


I fight death like a child fights aging. The end is near, I walk peacefully, relying only on myself.


I was a once soldier. I've seen death before. I have to keep my mind clear and not be disturbed by anything.


Death is inevitable. But I postpone it. Even steel breaks in the cold. The road is long, the journey is long.


After the darkest night, the sun shines brightest. The darkest night will usher in the brightest dawn.


I have become like steel. The wounds do not bother me but give me a little strength.


Blood flows in the soil. The petals must wither. The soul blooms like a wild flower after the fire.


On the mountain appeared the nameless field I longed for. Along the field, I saw a flame that looked like hope burning.


A pair of murderous eyes. Use them to help us achieve victory. Strength is the gift of the fearless. It always will be so.


I have sacrificed so much, and now I will take it all back. The glorious end is near. Go forward, my friend. To victory.
